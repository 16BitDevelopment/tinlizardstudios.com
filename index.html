Home

"This is my homeland: The Lizard Penninsular in Cornwall. Although I have adopted a lizard as my logo, the word Lizard in the old Cornish language means Palace or High Cliffs."

"I must have wandered all of this beautiful landscape over the years; sitting for hours dreaming, gazing out to sea on those rugged towering windswept cliffs on carpets of Heather & Thyme; seeking shelter in those secluded coves & in the secret valleys with lovely mosses & ferns growing by the streams in the woodlands."

Nowadays I share my home with very different flora & fauna, on top of a hill at Bentley, among the beautiful forests & mountain ranges of the wonderful Northern Rivers of NSW.

"I hope my artwork tells the story... the Natural world is my home & Nature is my kin."
